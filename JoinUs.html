We are Explorers | We are Soldiers

Our Mission is to promote Education and rise Social Awareness among the students of new Era and help them succeed in life in a path that we walked

Who We Are

EXPLORERS is basically a nonprofit student's organization vastly growing up in the area of Tongi,Gazipur.This organization is different from any other student organization within this context.Our main goal is to unite the students of this locality who are studying now in different universities around the country.At the same time, EXPLORERS offers the secondary and intermediate students the guideline to achieve their goal in Board exams and getting admission in renowned public as well as private universities of the country.As for the record,we,the organizers, had just got admitted in university, so we believe we are capable of sharing the idea and latest techniques to get into any Public Varsity.This area has produced lots of succesful academic,scholars and highly educated personals.But due not many reasons we do not get chances to meet our legends.Explorers hope to create chances to meet and gain knowledge from them in sooner future.

Our aim

  • Raise awareness among the students
  • Guide the young generation for a better future
  • Motivate the young generation from their depression
  • Help the young ones to face the real world and make them fit for it.
  • Inform the young generation about the versatile career opportunity
  • Make the young generation evolute in the advanced world and many more.

Our mission

  • Spread primary education among the underprivileged children
  • Engage in various social activities
  • Arrange study tours for free
  • Organize blood donation camps in educational institutes
  • Help poor students providing necessary educational materials
  • Ensure female higher education
  • Arranging career building and motivational seminars and many more
